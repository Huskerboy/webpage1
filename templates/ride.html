The rules of the game are quite simple. You will be making a series of guesses (4 in total). If you guess right, then you move on to the next part of the challenge, if you guess it wrong then you will drink. There are a few certain instances where you can get 'screwed' and you have to drink twice. It should also be noted that if you guess wrong, you must go back and start over at the beginning.
